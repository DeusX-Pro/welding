Heavy Duty Dewatering Presses

Our Hydraulic Presses are fabricated with thick channel iron frames to withstand immense pressure. Primarily used for de-watering cassava mash before frying, or extracting Palm Oil, these presses are essential for any agro-processing line.

We offer both manual screw presses and hydraulic jack systems depending on your budget and capacity needs.

Types Available:

  • Double Screw Manual Press: Economical and reliable.
  • Hydraulic Jack Press: High pressure for faster dewatering.
  • Capacity: Can hold 10-20 bags per batch.
